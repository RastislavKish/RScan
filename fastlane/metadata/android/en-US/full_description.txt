<p>
RScan allows blind and visually impaired people to quickly recognize any product simply by scanning its barcode!
</p>
<p>
Using a handy local caching system, users can collect relevant descriptions of their day to day used products and save them to a database, so they can hear the most important information in matter of seconds.
</p>
<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
<li>Support for EAN-13, EAN-8, UPC-A and UPC-E barcode formats.</li>
<li>A local barcode caching system, allowing the user to describe a product in any way they want.</li>
<li>Integrated tools for product identification, including searches from DuckDuckGo and a word selector for minimalization of the keyboard usage.</li>
</ul>
